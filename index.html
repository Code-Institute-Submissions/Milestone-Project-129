Virtual Barber Shop: 3D Sound

Experience the mind blowing effect of 3D sound in this recreation of having a haircut at a barber shop. The virtual placement of sound can be clearly heard all around you capturing frequencies from all spectrums. Make sure to use headphones!

Walking in the Rain in Manhattan, NYC: Binaural Audio

Take a trip around the bustling surroundings on Manhattan, with sound so real you would think you were there. This recording makes use of the Binaural recording technique, which involves using two microphones arranged in a way that creates a 3D sound space. Make sure to use headphones!
